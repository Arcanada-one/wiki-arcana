# Security policy

Report vulnerabilities privately to security@arcanada.ai. Do not open a public issue for an undisclosed vulnerability.

We acknowledge reports within 72 hours and aim to triage within 7 days. High and critical findings target remediation within 90 days; medium findings within 180 days. Coordinated disclosure occurs within 14 days after a fix or 120 days after the report, whichever is sooner.

Include affected versions, reproduction steps, impact, and any suggested mitigation. Never include live credentials or personal data in a report.
